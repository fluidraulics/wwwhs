fluidraulics, inc.

We are a value added fluid power distributor offering a wide range of pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric actuator products to OEMs, special machine builders, and end user accounts.

About Us

We have been serving our customers in the regional and surrounding areas of Pennsylvania and Ohio since 1967. Our experienced and knowledgeable employees are ready to assist you in choosing the correct product for your application as well as providing assistance in implementation of our vendor's products.

  • Learn more

Warehouses & Offices

Both warehouses and offices are conveniently located close to major highways and either the Ohio or Pennsylvania Turnpikes. Each warehouse utilizes automated shipping software and has dedicated areas for light assembly work. Both locations have daily UPS pickup and loading docks for larger freight shipments.

  • Learn more

Order Processing

We run industry accepted ERP systems in order to process your orders quickly and efficiently. Many orders are transmitted via EDI which offers a degree of accuracy not available with many other systems. We are also continually updating and refining our systems to keep pace with our customer’s needs.
